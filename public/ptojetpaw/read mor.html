Myrecipes: A Recipe Sharing Website Created by Computer Science Students :

Bessaa Abderraouf and Cherfa Hanna Sanna are two computer science students at the University of Algiers. They are passionate about cooking and decided to create a recipe sharing website to share their recipes with the world.

The website, called "Myrecipes", offers a variety of features that allow users to find, share, and create recipes.

Search :

Users can search for recipes by category, ingredient, or keyword. They can also filter search results by criteria such as difficulty, preparation time, or calorie count.

Add Recipes :

Users can add their own recipes to the website by filling out a form. The form asks users to provide information about the recipe, such as the name, category, ingredients, instructions, and photos.

Comments and Ratings :

Users can comment and rate other users' recipes. This allows users to share their opinions about recipes and find the best recipes.

Profile :

Users can create a profile on the website. Their profile allows them to store their recipes, follow other users, and receive notifications about their friends' activities.
